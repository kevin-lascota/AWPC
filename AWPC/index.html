Services

We're open during Monday, Wednesday, Friday from 11:00 AM to 7:00 PM and Saturday from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

Physical Therapy Session

Duration: 45 minutes | Price: 1,000 - 1,500 per session

Inclusive of Therapeutic Activities and Exercises, Manual therapy, Vibrocassor, Neuromuscular reeducation, Dry Needling, Acupuncture, Chiropractic Spinal Manipulation, RF Ultrasound, Microcurrent Therapy, Laser, TENS.

Microcurrent Therapy

Duration: Varies | Price: 500 per session

Uses extremely low-level electrical currents (microcurrents) to treat nerve and muscle pain, inflammation and other health challenges. Restores normal frequencies within the cells, resulting in remarkable improvements in pain, inflammation and function.

Laser Therapy

Duration: Varies | Price: 500 per session

Laser Therapy treatment is a non-invasive therapy that makes use of intense beams of light of specific wavelengths to help reduce pain related to your injury. Effective for pain relief â€“ reduces swelling from bruising or inflammation.

Chiropractic Spinal Manipulation

Duration: Varies | Price: 500 per session

Chiropractic Spinal Manipulation is a scientifically based field of health care which restores and maintains health and wellness of the entire body by focusing on the spine and nervous system.

Dry Needling

Duration: 10 minutes | Price: 300 per session

Intended to release tension, inflammation, chemicals or pressure on specific trigger points that cause muscle pain. Dry Needling Benefits 1. Reduces pain 2. Improves movement 3. Speeds up the recovery process

Acupuncture

Duration: Varies | Price: 300 per session

This form of treatment has roots in Traditional Chinese Medicine and operates with the idea that every form of illness is caused by an energy imbalance in the human body.

Therapeutic RF (radiofrequency) Ultrasound

Duration: Varies | Price: 300 per session

The most common conditions treated with ultrasound include soft tissue injuries such as tendonitis (or tendinitis if you prefer), non-acute joint swelling and muscle spasm. Most muscle and ligament injuries can benefit from therapeutic ultrasound.

Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation (TENS)

Duration: 15 minutes | Price: 300 per session

TENS is a non-invasive way to override or block signals from the nerves to the spinal cord and brain. Provide temporary or even long-lasting pain relief. Can also improve local circulation and reduce or eliminate muscle spasm.
